Khelsetu Platform

Khelsetu is a digital platform launched by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports this App is provided on an “as is” basis without guarantees of uninterrupted service or accuracy. This is for the selection of the athlete in (SAI) by online method with some use of AI tools

  • Athletes from rural & urban areas get equal opportunity
  • Sports organizations get a larger talent pool
  • Talent identification platform
  • Reduces cost of physical trials & scouting Opens new career opportunities for young athletes.
  • Training program management
